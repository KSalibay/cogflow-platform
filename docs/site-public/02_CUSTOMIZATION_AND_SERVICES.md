# Customization and Services

This page describes what can be extended or commissioned beyond the core open-source platform.

## What Can Be Customized

- Task/plugin behavior and parameter surfaces
- Experiment authoring templates and defaults
- Runtime integrations and result handling paths
- Study governance workflows
- Data export and downstream analytics handoff
- UI/UX branding and lab-specific operational flows

## Typical Service Engagements

- New behavioral task implementation
- Existing task adaptation for a specific paradigm
- Study pipeline setup for institutional environments
- Deployment hardening and operational readiness
- Research staff onboarding and workflow training

## Engagement Model (Public-Facing)

- Core platform remains open and free to use
- Additional services are scoped to project requirements
- Deliverables can include implementation, validation, documentation, and support

## Suggested Website CTA Copy

Need a custom task or a lab-specific workflow? CogFlow can be extended to fit your protocol, infrastructure, and governance requirements while keeping your core research stack open and transparent.
