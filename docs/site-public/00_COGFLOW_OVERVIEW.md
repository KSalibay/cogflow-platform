# What Is CogFlow?

CogFlow is an open-source platform for building, running, and managing behavioral experiments.

It is designed for psychologists and behavioral researchers who need:
- Flexible experiment authoring
- Reliable study runtime
- Researcher-focused operations and governance
- Strong privacy and audit controls for participant data

## What CogFlow Does

- Builder authoring workflow for task configuration
- Interpreter runtime for study execution
- Portal for study lifecycle and run monitoring
- API-driven integration between components
- Export and deployment options for local and platform-backed workflows

## Why Teams Choose CogFlow

- Open-source foundation with transparent architecture
- Free core platform with no forced license lock-in for your research workflow
- Extensive customization capacity for protocols, integrations, and operational needs
- Research-ready privacy posture with encrypted payload handling and auditability

## Best-Fit Users

CogFlow is best suited to:
- Academic psychology labs
- Clinical and cognitive research teams
- Applied behavioral science groups
- Organizations that need custom task pipelines and controlled data governance
